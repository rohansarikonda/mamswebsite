Examining the Use of Hypervectors and Fuzzy Signatures in Biometric Authentication

Importance

With this project, the future of biometric authentication can be further examined. While current biometric authentication methods are well-developed, they are beginning to hit their ceilings, and new systems are beginning to need to be examined. This project delves into this issue in an attempt to solve this problem.
